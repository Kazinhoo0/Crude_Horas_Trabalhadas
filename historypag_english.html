About us

It is a project that seeks to help programmers get organized with the time they spend on each project and opens the door to other features. padprojects provides a CRUD of actions to organize your projects, in it you can attach your project, and add how many hours you worked in the day on the given project. The goal is for you to add how many hours you worked each day, and at the end analyze how long you worked. In it, you can also include the hourly rate if you're working on a freelance project, for example. The idea is for you to have at hand the control of the hours you work on each project and a crud of information.
